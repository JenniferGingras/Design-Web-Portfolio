Ignite Conference Program

Objective \
To create the Ignite conference program guide to work as a digital pdf and a printed booklet.

The structure of the booklet needed to be versatile enough to accommodate a variety of information which included a visual schedule, the details of talks and events, and speaker biographies. The conference took place in Santa Ana Pueblo, New Mexico which allowed us to have some fun with adding a southwest flair to some of the design elements, while still staying true to the IGNITE brand.
