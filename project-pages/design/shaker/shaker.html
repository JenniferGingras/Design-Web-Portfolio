Identity Rebranding

Giving new purpose to an old company.



Shaker Urban Garden

Objective \
Taking a dead, dying or defunct brand and reviving it with a new purpose, audience and identity.

The Shaker Seed Company was innovative in its time, as the first company to sell seed packets to individual consumers. Though the company was dismantled 100 years ago, it is the perfect time to re-envision it for contemporary times. By changing the audience from rural folks making a self-sufficient living to urban dwellers embracing sustainable lifestyles I shifted the opportunities and the scope of the brand without losing the original mission and heart. The brand book lays out the new vision and visual guidelines.
